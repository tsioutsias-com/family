The three brothers, Tsioutsias or Kasimis, were summoned by the Aga to his home at the castle of Mila.

Only the first and second went. The Aga captured them and dragged each by a hook from their neck to their back and let them go.

The first brother died from bleeding before reaching Kouvella, where they lived.

The second did not die then, but years later from an infection after being bitten by a wildcat while carrying a stolen cow. (From him descends Giorgakis the "Kontovounisios")

The third brother, short and stocky, took revenge by killing the Aga. We are descended from him.
